JPJePlate in Johor Bahru: what drivers need to know

A practical guide for drivers preparing to order and install an electronic number plate in JB.

JPJePlate is the electronic number plate system used for eligible Malaysian vehicles. For Johor Bahru drivers, the main things to understand are the order requirements, delivery timing, and the need for professional installation.

How the JB installation flow works

The order starts online through WhatsApp. After your details and documents are checked, the eplate is ordered and delivered to the Johor Bahru workshop. Once it arrives, you book an installation slot and bring the vehicle in for fitting.

What to prepare before ordering

  • Your vehicle registration number.
  • Your chassis number.
  • MyKad photo for identity verification.
  • Vehicle grant photo for ownership and registration details.

Why installation should be done by an installer

Professional fitting helps ensure the front and rear plates are mounted correctly and that the order remains aligned with JPJePlate requirements. It also gives you one clear contact point if you need follow-up support.
