JPJePlate Basics

What is JPJePlate?

A clear guide to Malaysia's electronic number plate system for drivers preparing to order.

JPJePlate is Malaysia's electronic vehicle registration plate system. It was introduced to create a more standardised, secure, and traceable number plate format for vehicles covered by the programme.

Why JPJePlate exists

Traditional number plates can vary in font, spacing, size, and build quality. A standardised plate system helps make vehicle identification clearer and more consistent. It also supports better enforcement, improves visibility, and reduces the risk of counterfeit or cloned plates.

What makes it different?

  • Standardised plate design and format.
  • Anti-counterfeit characteristics.
  • RFID readiness for future intelligent transport use cases.
  • Professional fitting through recognised channels.

What drivers should prepare

Before ordering, prepare your registration number, chassis number, MyKad photo, and vehicle grant details. Accurate information reduces back-and-forth and helps the order move faster.
