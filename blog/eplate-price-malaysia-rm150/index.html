How much does an eplate cost in Malaysia?

A simple breakdown of the official JPJePlate price and the RM150 ePlate.my installed package.

The official JPJePlate FAQ lists the standard plate set at RM98. That figure is useful when you are comparing the base plate cost, but it does not always represent the full amount a driver pays when using a local service that handles order assistance, updates, workshop coordination, and fitting.

RM98

Official JPJePlate plate set price listed in the JPJePlate FAQ.

RM150

ePlate.my service package shown on this website for standard passenger vehicles, covering local handling, WhatsApp updates, delivery coordination to the JB workshop, and professional installation.

What can be included in an installed package?

  • Front and rear electronic number plates.
  • Order guidance and document checking.
  • Delivery updates through WhatsApp.
  • Installation appointment at the Johor Bahru workshop.
  • Official invoice after payment.

What can affect timing?

Timing depends on document completeness, order queue, public holidays, and delivery to the workshop. Most drivers should prepare for several working days between order submission and fitting.
