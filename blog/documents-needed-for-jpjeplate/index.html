Documents needed to order a JPJePlate

Prepare these details before you WhatsApp your order so the process is faster.

Ordering a JPJePlate is easier when your documents are ready before you contact the installer. Clear photos and accurate details help prevent delays.

Basic checklist

  • MyKad photo
    Used for identity verification during the order process.
  • Vehicle grant photo
    Used to confirm registration, owner, and vehicle details.
  • Registration number
    Send the exact number shown on your vehicle record.
  • Chassis number
    Needed to match the eplate order to the correct vehicle.

Photo tips

Take photos in good lighting, avoid glare, and make sure all text is readable. If a photo is blurry, the installer may need to ask again, which slows the order down.

Privacy note

Only send documents through the contact channel you trust. ePlate.my uses WhatsApp for order handling and updates, so keep the conversation in one thread for easier tracking.
