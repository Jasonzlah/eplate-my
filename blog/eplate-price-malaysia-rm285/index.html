How much does an eplate cost in Malaysia?

A simple breakdown of the RM285 eplate package and what drivers should check before ordering.

The eplate price shown on ePlate.my is RM285 per vehicle. The goal is to keep the package simple so drivers know what is included before they order.

RM285

Per standard passenger vehicle, including front and rear electronic plates, order handling, delivery to the JB workshop, and professional installation.

What is included?

  • Front and rear electronic number plates.
  • JPJePlate registration and order processing.
  • Delivery updates through WhatsApp.
  • Installation appointment at the Johor Bahru workshop.
  • Official invoice after payment.

What can affect timing?

Timing depends on document completeness, order queue, public holidays, and delivery to the workshop. Most drivers should prepare for several working days between order submission and fitting.

Before you pay

Confirm the vehicle details, installation location, payment method, and whether your vehicle type is covered by the standard package.
