How to choose a JPJePlate installer in Malaysia

What drivers should ask before paying for eplate ordering and installation.

A good JPJePlate installer should make the process clear before you send documents or make payment. The right questions help you avoid confusion about pricing, timing, and installation.

Questions to ask first

  • What is included in the price?
  • Is installation included or charged separately?
  • Where is the workshop located?
  • What documents are required?
  • How will I receive order and delivery updates?
  • Will I receive an invoice or receipt?

Check the price clearly

The official JPJePlate plate set price and a local installed service package are not always the same thing. Ask for a written breakdown so you know whether the price includes order handling, delivery coordination, and fitting.

Choose convenience, but keep records

WhatsApp ordering is convenient, but keep your payment proof, invoice, appointment details, and any messages about installation. These records are useful if you need support later.
