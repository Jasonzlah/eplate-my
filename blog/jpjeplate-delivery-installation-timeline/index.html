JPJePlate delivery and installation timeline

What to expect after you start your eplate order.

The JPJePlate process has several steps. Most delays happen when documents are unclear, payment is not confirmed, or the driver is not available for installation after the plate arrives.

Typical order flow

  1. Contact the installer and send your vehicle details.
  2. Submit MyKad and vehicle grant photos for checking.
  3. Confirm package price, payment method, and installation location.
  4. The order is submitted and processed.
  5. The plate is delivered to the workshop.
  6. You book a fitting slot and bring the vehicle in.

How long does it take?

Delivery timing can vary depending on order volume, document accuracy, courier timing, weekends, and public holidays. ePlate.my keeps customers updated through WhatsApp so they know when to expect the next step.

How to avoid delays

Send clear photos, confirm your chassis number carefully, respond quickly to payment or document questions, and keep your installation appointment once the plate arrives.
