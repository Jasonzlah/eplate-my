FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does delivery take? +

JPJePlate typically ships to our Johor Bahru workshop within 3–7 working days after your order is placed. We'll notify you via WhatsApp the moment it arrives.

Do I need to come to your workshop to order? +

No — the entire ordering process is done via WhatsApp. You only visit our JB workshop once, for the installation appointment.

What documents do I need? +

You need your MyKad (NRIC) and your vehicle grant (geran kenderaan). Photos taken on your phone and sent via WhatsApp are acceptable.

Is the eplate JPJ-registered and legal? +

Yes. Every eplate is officially registered through the JPJePlate portal and is fully JPJ-compliant. You'll receive a registration certificate.

What areas do you serve? +

We serve all of Johor and southern Malaysia. Our installation workshop is in Johor Bahru, easily accessible from major highways.

What if I have issues after installation? +

WhatsApp us and we'll arrange a follow-up visit or replacement at no extra charge. We stand behind our work.

Can I install the eplate myself? +

No — JPJePlate requires professional installation by an authorised installer. DIY installation will void your registration.

Do you accept credit cards? +

We accept bank transfer, online banking (FPX), and e-wallets. Payment details will be sent via WhatsApp after you place your order.
